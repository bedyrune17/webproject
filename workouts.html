Workout tracks

Each track is built around a clear outcome and a repeatable week.

Strength foundation

Squat, press, hinge, and pull patterns with progressive loading and technique checkpoints.

  • Best for
    Beginners and members who want measurable strength gains.
  • Weekly flow
    3 to 4 sessions with core lifts and accessory work.

Conditioning and fat loss

Resistance training plus short interval work to improve stamina and total calorie burn.

  • Best for
    Members who want fat loss without dropping all strength work.
  • Weekly flow
    3 lift sessions, 2 HIIT blocks, and step targets.

Mobility and recovery

Low-impact movement, mobility circuits, and recovery-focused training for consistency.

  • Best for
    Busy students, deconditioned members, and deload phases.
  • Weekly flow
    2 to 3 sessions with mobility, core stability, and easy conditioning.
Class rhythm

Sample class schedule

Monday: HIIT Blast at 7:00 AM

Saturday: Core and Mobility at 9:00 AM
  • Beginner split
    3 full-body sessions each week with walking and mobility work.
  • Fat-loss split
    3 lifting days, 2 conditioning sessions, and calorie tracking.
  • Muscle-gain split
    4 to 5 lifting days with steady surplus and sleep focus.
