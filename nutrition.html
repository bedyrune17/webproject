Nutrition

Food strategies matched to training goals

The nutrition side of the project is built around clear, realistic paths rather than complicated meal rules.

  1. Weight loss diet with calorie awareness and simple portion rules
  2. Muscle gain diet with a manageable surplus and protein targets
  3. Vegetarian plans focused on adherence and protein quality
  4. High-protein templates for performance and appetite control
Coaching logic

What makes a plan sustainable

  • Simple meal templates
    Members can repeat what works instead of rebuilding every day.
  • Adjustments over perfection
    Calories and portions are reviewed based on real progress.
  • Goal-first structure
    Meals support training, recovery, and consistency, not just restriction.
