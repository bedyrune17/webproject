Body Mass Index (BMI)

Enter your weight and height to calculate BMI and see a broad health category.

Note

How to read the result

  • Useful for screening
    BMI is a quick estimate based on height and weight.
  • Not the full picture
    It does not separately measure fat mass, muscle mass, or athletic build.
  • Best used with context
    Combine BMI with progress photos, measurements, energy levels, and training performance.
