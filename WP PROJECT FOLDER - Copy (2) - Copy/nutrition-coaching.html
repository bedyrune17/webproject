Nutrition Coaching

Structured nutrition guidance to support fat loss, muscle gain or maintenance.

How it works

  1. Intake: we gather your history, preferences, allergies and schedule.
  2. Analysis: calorie needs estimated and baseline diet reviewed.
  3. Plan: personalised meal templates and sample days based on your goal.
  4. Support: weekly check-ins and adjustments, recipe suggestions, and portion guidance.

Packages

  • Starter (4 weeks): baseline plan and 2 check-ins — ₹1200
  • Performance (8 weeks): full plan, weekly check-ins, macro targets — ₹2200
  • Premium: includes body composition tracking and weekly coaching — ₹4000

Sample recommendations

Example: For muscle gain a starting macronutrient split might be 30% protein, 25% fats, 45% carbs with a +10-15% calorie surplus; for cutting we lean towards higher protein and a -15-20% deficit.
