Nutrition coaching

Structured guidance for fat loss, muscle gain, or maintenance

This service turns the general nutrition page into a coached process with feedback and adjustments.

How it works

  1. Intake: collect preferences, allergies, routine, and current habits.
  2. Analysis: estimate calorie needs and review the current diet pattern.
  3. Plan: create meal templates and goal-specific sample days.
  4. Support: check in weekly and adjust portions, food choices, and adherence strategies.
