Nutrition paths

Each path keeps food choices tied to the goal, schedule, and training load.

Fat loss structure

Moderate calorie deficit, high protein, and repeatable meals that fit a class schedule.

  • Focus
    Adherence, hunger control, and steady weekly progress.
  • Best tool
    Daily calorie calculator for a starting target.

Muscle gain structure

Small calorie surplus, protein timing, and easy snacks that support performance and recovery.

  • Focus
    Training performance, recovery, and manageable appetite load.
  • Best fit
    Members running 4 to 5 lifting sessions each week.

Maintenance and lifestyle

Flexible meal templates that keep energy and recovery stable without obsessing over every gram.

  • Focus
    Routine, consistency, and long-term sustainability.
  • Best fit
    Members who want structure without aggressive dieting.
Sample day

Simple student-friendly meal flow

  • Breakfast
    Oats or idlis with eggs, paneer, or curd for protein.
  • Lunch
    Rice or roti with dal, vegetables, and a main protein source.
  • Pre/post workout
    Fruit, milk, whey, or a sandwich based on schedule.
  • Dinner
    Repeat the same structure with lighter portions if needed.
Coaching logic
  • Simple meal templates
    Members repeat what works instead of rebuilding every day.
  • Adjustments over perfection
    Calories and portions are reviewed based on real outcomes.
  • Goal-first structure
    Meals support training, recovery, appetite, and routine.
