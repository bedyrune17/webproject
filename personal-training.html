What we offer

One-to-one coaching focused on safe progress, individual programming, and measurable results.

Procedure

  1. Assessment: movement screen, strength and cardiovascular baseline, diet & lifestyle review.
  2. Goal plan: personalised 8–12 week program with weekly targets and session plans.
  3. Training sessions: in-gym or online sessions (coach-led), technique coaching, progress tracking.
  4. Review & adjust: bi-weekly check-ins and program adjustments based on progress.
